How it works

Live in three moves.

No new hardware. No member app. No per-message bill.

  1. 01

    Link your WhatsApp

    Scan one QR code from your gym phone. The bot rides on your real number, so members chat the contact they already know.

  2. 02

    Drop in your details

    Plans, prices, hours, trainers, facilities. One simple file, no code to touch. Change it any time.

  3. 03

    Let it run

    It greets, books, reminds and renews while you coach. Glance at the daily digest, step in only when a human is needed.
