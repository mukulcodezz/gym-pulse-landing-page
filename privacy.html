Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GymPulse ("we", "us", "our") operates a WhatsApp automation service for gym businesses. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

When a gym member interacts with a GymPulse-powered WhatsApp bot, we collect:

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Name (provided during onboarding)
  • Fitness goals, age, and preferred training time (provided during onboarding)
  • Trial booking details (date, time slot)
  • Check-in timestamps
  • Membership plan and expiry information (entered by the gym)
  • Conversation messages sent to the bot

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond the above.

2. How we use your data

  • Responding to enquiries and FAQs via the bot
  • Booking and confirming free trial sessions
  • Sending membership renewal reminders
  • Logging gym check-ins
  • Sending re-engagement messages to inactive members
  • Providing gym owners with operational digests (member names, expiry dates)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

3. AI processing

Questions that fall outside scripted responses are processed by third-party AI providers. Your message text may be sent to one or more of the following:

  • Groq (groq.com) — privacy policy at groq.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini (google.com) — privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) — privacy policy at openrouter.ai/privacy

These providers process messages solely to generate a reply. We do not share your name, phone number, or any identifying information alongside the message text.

4. WhatsApp / Meta

GymPulse operates over WhatsApp. All conversations are subject to Meta's privacy policy and WhatsApp's terms of service. We do not control how Meta processes messages transmitted through its platform.

5. Data storage

Data is stored on servers controlled by the gym owner or GymPulse. We use industry-standard practices (encrypted storage, access controls) to protect stored data. Conversation-level AI responses are cached for up to 7 days, then automatically deleted.

6. Data retention

Member data is retained for as long as the gym maintains an active GymPulse subscription. On subscription termination, data can be exported and/or deleted upon written request. Gym owners may delete individual contact records at any time.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact the gym you interacted with — they are the data controller for their members' information. For GymPulse-level data concerns, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to gym owners. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
